    About

    We founded RedPunch Studios, a local young game company, together with Bertan, İlkan and Tayfun on January 8, 2020. Our aim is to gain a place in the Turkish game industry and to open up to the Global Market.

    Our Goal

    To bring the Turkish game industry to better places.

    Our Plan

    Our plan is to release our first two games "Project: Hell" and "Endless Dungeon" on the specified dates.

    Our Projects

    We are currently working on our Project: Hell game. You can follow the developments on Twitter at @projecthellgame.
